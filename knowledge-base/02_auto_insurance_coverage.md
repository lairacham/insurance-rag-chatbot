# ShieldBase Auto Insurance — Coverage Details

## Overview

ShieldBase Auto Insurance protects you on the road with flexible plans designed for every driver. Whether you're a daily commuter, weekend driver, or road-trip enthusiast, we have a plan that fits your needs and budget.

## Coverage Tiers

### 1. Basic (Liability-Only)
Meets state minimum requirements. Recommended for older vehicles with low market value.

- **Bodily Injury Liability:** Up to $50,000 per person / $100,000 per accident
- **Property Damage Liability:** Up to $25,000 per accident
- **Uninsured Motorist Coverage:** Included
- Does NOT cover damage to your own vehicle

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$62/month

---

### 2. Standard
Adds protection for your own vehicle in collision scenarios.

Includes everything in Basic, plus:
- **Collision Coverage:** Repairs or replaces your vehicle after an at-fault accident
- **Medical Payments Coverage:** Up to $5,000 per person for injury-related medical expenses
- **Rental Reimbursement:** Up to $40/day while your car is being repaired (max 30 days)

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$118/month

---

### 3. Comprehensive (Full Coverage)
Our most complete plan. Covers virtually all incidents.

Includes everything in Standard, plus:
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** Damage from theft, vandalism, fire, flooding, hail, and hitting an animal
- **Roadside Assistance:** Towing, battery jump-start, flat tire change, lockout service
- **Gap Coverage (optional add-on):** Pays the difference between your car's actual cash value and your remaining loan balance if your car is totaled
- **New Car Replacement (optional add-on):** If your vehicle is less than 2 years old and totaled, we replace it with a brand-new equivalent model

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$189/month

---

## What Is NOT Covered

- Commercial or rideshare use (requires a separate commercial auto policy)
- Mechanical breakdown or regular maintenance (oil changes, tire wear, etc.)
- Intentional damage caused by the policyholder
- Racing or track events
- Vehicles driven outside the U.S. or Canada without prior written approval
- Personal belongings left in the vehicle (covered under home/renters insurance)

## Key Auto Insurance Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Deductible** | Amount you pay out-of-pocket before insurance kicks in. Typical options: $250, $500, $1,000 |
| **Premium** | Monthly or annual cost of the policy |
| **Liability** | Coverage for damage or injury you cause to others |
| **Collision** | Coverage for damage to your car in a crash, regardless of fault |
| **Comprehensive** | Coverage for non-collision damage (theft, weather, animals, etc.) |
| **Actual Cash Value (ACV)** | Market value of your car at time of loss, accounting for depreciation |

## Factors That Affect Your Auto Premium

- **Driver age** — Drivers under 25 and over 75 may pay higher premiums
- **Driving history** — Accidents, DUIs, or speeding tickets increase rates
- **Vehicle make, model, and year** — Newer, more expensive vehicles cost more to insure
- **Annual mileage** — Higher mileage = higher risk exposure
- **Location** — Urban areas typically have higher rates due to theft and accident frequency
- **Credit score** (where permitted by state law)
- **Coverage level and deductible chosen**
