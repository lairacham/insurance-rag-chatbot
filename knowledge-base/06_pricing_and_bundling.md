# ShieldBase Insurance — Pricing, Tiers & Bundling

## Overview

ShieldBase offers transparent, competitive pricing across all three product lines. Premiums are calculated based on your individual risk profile, selected coverage level, and applicable discounts. Below are reference starting rates and bundling options.

---

## Auto Insurance — Reference Pricing

Rates shown are monthly starting premiums for a 30-year-old driver with a clean record, insuring a 2020 mid-size sedan in a suburban area.

| Tier | Coverage Type | Starting Monthly Premium |
|---|---|---|
| Basic | Liability only | ~$62/month |
| Standard | Liability + Collision | ~$118/month |
| Comprehensive | Full Coverage | ~$189/month |

**Deductible options:** $250 / $500 / $1,000
Higher deductible = lower premium. Choosing a $1,000 deductible over $250 can reduce your Comprehensive premium by up to 18%.

## Bundling Discounts

ShieldBase rewards customers who consolidate their insurance needs with us. Bundle two or more products to unlock significant savings.

| Bundle Combination | Discount |
|---|---|
| Auto + Home | Up to 15% off each policy |
| Auto + Life | Up to 10% off each policy |
| Home + Life | Up to 10% off each policy |
| Auto + Home + Life (Triple Bundle) | Up to 20% off each policy |

**Example savings:**
A customer paying $189/month (auto) + $130/month (home) = $319/month. With a 15% bundle discount, they pay ~$271/month — saving $576/year.

## Payment Options

| Payment Frequency | Surcharge/Discount |
|---|---|
| Monthly (auto-pay) | No surcharge |
| Monthly (manual) | +3% processing fee |
| Semi-annual (every 6 months) | 2% discount |

Auto-pay is available via bank account (ACH) or credit/debit card. No convenience fees for ACH payments.

---

## Premium Review and Rate Changes

- Premiums are reviewed at each policy renewal (annually for most policies)
- Rate increases require 30 days' advance written notice
- Filing a claim may affect your premium at renewal, depending on claim type and history
- Improving your credit score, completing a defensive driving course, or adding security features to your home can reduce your rates mid-term — contact your agent to request a re-evaluation
