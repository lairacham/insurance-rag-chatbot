# ShieldBase Insurance — Claims Process & Policies

## How to File a Claim

ShieldBase makes filing a claim as simple as possible. You can submit a claim through any of the following channels:

1. **Online Portal:** Log in at www.shieldbaseinsurance.com/claims — available 24/7
2. **Mobile App:** Use the ShieldBase app to photograph damage and submit instantly
3. **Claims Hotline:** Call 1-800-743-5528 — available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
4. **In-Person:** Visit any ShieldBase regional office or authorized agent location

## Step-by-Step Claims Process

### Step 1 — Report the Incident
File your claim as soon as possible after an incident. Most policies require claims to be reported within **30 days** of the loss. Delayed reporting may affect your claim eligibility.

### Step 2 — Receive a Claim Number
You'll receive a unique claim reference number immediately after filing. Use this number for all follow-up communications.

### Step 3 — Assigned Adjuster
A licensed claims adjuster will be assigned to your case within **1 business day**. They will contact you to schedule an inspection (for auto or home claims) or review documentation (for life claims).

### Step 4 — Damage Assessment / Documentation Review
- **Auto claims:** An adjuster inspects the vehicle in person or via photo submission. Repair estimates are obtained from approved repair shops.
- **Home claims:** An adjuster visits the property or reviews contractor estimates.
- **Life claims:** Beneficiaries submit the death certificate, completed claim form, and policy documents.

### Step 5 — Claim Decision
ShieldBase provides a claim decision within **3–5 business days** of completing the assessment. Complex or disputed claims may take up to 30 days.

### Step 6 — Payment
Approved claims are paid by:
- **Direct deposit** (fastest — 1–2 business days after approval)
- **Check by mail** (5–7 business days)
- **Direct payment to repair shop or contractor** (for auto/home claims)

## Deductibles

Your deductible is the amount you pay out-of-pocket before ShieldBase covers the remaining loss.

- **Auto:** Deductibles typically range from $250 to $1,000. You choose your deductible when purchasing the policy. A higher deductible lowers your monthly premium.
- **Home:** Deductibles typically range from $500 to $5,000. Some policies have a separate wind/hail deductible expressed as a percentage of dwelling coverage (e.g., 1–2%).
- **Life:** No deductible — death benefits are paid in full upon approval.

**Note:** Deductibles apply per claim, not per year (except where noted in your specific policy terms).

## What Happens If Your Claim Is Denied

If your claim is denied, you will receive a written explanation citing the specific policy exclusion or reason. You have the right to:

1. **Request a Review:** Ask for a secondary internal review within 30 days of denial notice
2. **File a Formal Appeal:** Submit a written appeal with additional documentation within 60 days
3. **Escalate to Your State Insurance Commissioner** if the dispute is unresolved after the internal appeal

ShieldBase policyholders have a claims satisfaction rate of 94% and a denial rate of under 4%.

## Fraud Warning

Insurance fraud is a felony. Submitting false or inflated claims, staging accidents, or misrepresenting facts will result in immediate policy cancellation, full recovery of fraudulent payments, and referral to law enforcement.

## Tips for a Smooth Claim

- **Document everything** — Photos, videos, and written records before any cleanup or repairs
- **Keep receipts** — For any emergency repairs made to prevent further damage
- **Don't dispose of damaged property** until the adjuster has assessed it
- **File a police report** for theft, vandalism, hit-and-run, or any criminal activity
- **Notify us promptly** — Late filings can complicate or jeopardize your claim
