# ShieldBase Insurance — Policies, Eligibility & Special Programs

## Policy Terms and Renewal

### Policy Periods
- **Auto and Home:** 12-month policy terms, renewable annually
- **Life (Term):** Fixed term (10, 15, 20, or 30 years); renews at adjusted rate unless converted to whole life
- **Life (Whole):** Permanent — no renewal required as long as premiums are paid

### Renewal Process
Policies renew automatically unless you cancel. You will receive a renewal notice **45 days before** your expiration date showing any premium changes for the coming year. You have until the renewal date to make changes, cancel, or switch plans.

### Non-Renewal by ShieldBase
ShieldBase may choose not to renew a policy in cases of:
- Excessive claims (3 or more at-fault or preventable claims in 3 years)
- Material misrepresentation on the original application
- Non-payment of premium
- Change in risk profile that exceeds underwriting guidelines

---

## Cancellation Policy

**By the Policyholder:**
- Cancel at any time with no fee
- Pro-rated refund for unused premium (if paid annually or semi-annually)
- Cancellation effective immediately or on a future date you specify

**By ShieldBase:**
- Within the first 60 days: 10 days written notice for any reason
- After 60 days: 30 days written notice required; must cite specific reason per state law

---

## Grace Period and Reinstatement

- **Grace period:** 10 days after missed payment due date
- **Reinstatement window:** 30 days after lapse, with payment of overdue premium + $25 fee
- Policies lapsed more than 30 days require a new application and may be subject to re-underwriting

## Eligibility Requirements

### Auto Insurance
- Valid U.S. driver's license (learner's permits require co-insured licensed adult)
- Vehicle must be registered in the U.S.
- Vehicles used primarily for commercial purposes are not eligible (see Commercial Auto)
- No more than 3 at-fault accidents in the past 5 years (otherwise, high-risk program applies)

### Home Insurance
- Property must be located in the U.S.
- Homeowner's policy requires that you own and occupy the property as your primary residence
- Properties with active structural damage or code violations are not eligible until remediated
- Properties in FEMA-designated Special Flood Hazard Areas require a separate flood endorsement

### Life Insurance
- Minimum age: 18 years old
- Maximum age at application: 75 (term); 85 (whole life with limited benefit)
- U.S. citizen or permanent resident with a valid Social Security Number
- Medical exam required for coverage amounts above $500,000

## High-Risk Programs

Drivers or homeowners who don't qualify for standard plans may be eligible for ShieldBase's **Assured Coverage Program**, a non-standard plan designed for higher-risk applicants. Features include:
- Higher premiums reflecting elevated risk
- Same core coverage options (Basic, Standard, Comprehensive)
- 12-month policy with re-evaluation at renewal
- Eligible for rate improvement after 24 months of claims-free history

## Special Programs

### ShieldBase Drive Smart (Telematics — Auto)
Install the free ShieldBase app plug-in to track driving behavior. Safe drivers earn discounts of up to 25% based on:
- Braking patterns
- Speed
- Phone usage while driving
- Time of day driven

Enrollment is voluntary. Data is used only for discount calculation and is not shared with third parties.

### ShieldBase Home Protect Monitor (Smart Home — Home)
Connect your home security system or smart sensors to the ShieldBase portal. Participants receive:
- Up to 8% premium discount
- Real-time alerts for smoke, water leak, or intrusion
- Expedited claims processing for monitored incidents

### ShieldBase Life Rewards (Wellness — Life)
Policyholders who complete an annual wellness check and submit results through the portal may qualify for:
- Up to 5% premium reduction
- Access to a free nurse health line (24/7)
- Preferred rates if health metrics improve at renewal

## Privacy and Data Use

ShieldBase collects personal and vehicle/property data solely for underwriting, policy management, and claims processing. We comply with all applicable state privacy laws. You may request a copy of your data or ask us to delete non-essential data by contacting privacy@shieldbaseinsurance.com.

We do not sell or rent personal information to marketers or third parties.

## Complaints and Dispute Resolution

If you are dissatisfied with a policy decision or claims outcome:

1. Contact your assigned agent or call 1-800-743-5527
2. Request a formal internal review — response within 10 business days
3. File a written appeal — response within 30 business days
4. Escalate to your state's Department of Insurance if unresolved

ShieldBase is a member of the Insurance Dispute Resolution Service (IDRS) and participates in binding arbitration for eligible disputes.
