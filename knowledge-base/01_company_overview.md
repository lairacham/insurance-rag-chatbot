# ShieldBase Insurance — Company Overview

## Who We Are

ShieldBase Insurance is a modern, customer-first insurance provider founded in 2008 and headquartered in Austin, Texas. We serve over 2 million policyholders across the United States, offering reliable coverage backed by a financially stable, A-rated underwriting group.

Our mission is simple: make insurance straightforward, transparent, and accessible. We believe every person deserves protection that fits their life — without confusing jargon, hidden fees, or frustrating claims processes.

## What We Offer

ShieldBase provides three core insurance products:

- **Auto Insurance** — Coverage for personal vehicles including cars, trucks, and SUVs. We offer liability, collision, comprehensive, and full-coverage plans.
- **Home Insurance** — Protection for homeowners and renters against property damage, theft, natural disasters, and personal liability.
- **Life Insurance** — Term and whole life policies that provide financial security for your loved ones.

## Why Choose ShieldBase

- **Fast Claims** — Most claims are processed within 3–5 business days. Our online portal lets you file and track claims 24/7.
- **Flexible Bundling** — Bundle two or more policies and save up to 20% on your combined premium.
- **No Hidden Fees** — Transparent pricing with no enrollment fees or penalty charges for switching plans.
- **Dedicated Agents** — Every customer is assigned a personal account agent reachable by phone, email, or chat.
- **Digital-First Experience** — Manage your policy, pay bills, and submit claims entirely online or via the ShieldBase mobile app.

## Financial Strength

ShieldBase is backed by ShieldBase Holdings Group, which maintains an A (Excellent) rating from AM Best and an AA- rating from Standard & Poor's. Our claims-paying ability is among the strongest in the mid-size insurer segment.

## Licensing and Compliance

ShieldBase Insurance is licensed to operate in all 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia. We comply fully with state insurance regulations and are a member of the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC).
