# ShieldBase Home Insurance — Coverage Details

## Overview

Your home is likely your biggest investment. ShieldBase Home Insurance protects your property, possessions, and personal liability — so you can recover quickly when the unexpected happens. We cover homeowners, condo owners, and renters.

## Coverage Tiers

### 1. Basic (HO-1 Equivalent)
Named-peril coverage for primary structures. Suitable for low-risk properties or budget-conscious homeowners.

**Covered Perils:**
- Fire and smoke damage
- Lightning strikes
- Windstorm and hail
- Explosion
- Theft and vandalism
- Damage from vehicles or aircraft

**Coverage Limits:**
- Dwelling (Structure): Up to $300,000
- Personal Property: Up to $50,000
- Liability: $100,000 per occurrence

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$75/month

---

### 2. Standard (HO-3 Equivalent)
Open-peril coverage on the dwelling structure; named-peril on personal property. Our most popular home plan.

Includes everything in Basic, plus:
- **Water Damage** from burst pipes or accidental overflow (not flooding)
- **Falling Objects** (e.g., tree limb through roof)
- **Ice, Snow, or Sleet** structural damage
- **Loss of Use / Additional Living Expenses:** Up to $20,000 if your home becomes uninhabitable during repairs
- **Medical Payments to Others:** Up to $5,000 if a guest is injured on your property

**Coverage Limits:**
- Dwelling: Up to $600,000
- Personal Property: Up to $150,000
- Liability: $300,000 per occurrence

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$130/month

---

### 3. Comprehensive (HO-5 Equivalent)
Open-peril coverage on both the dwelling and personal property. Maximum protection.

Includes everything in Standard, plus:
- **High-Value Item Riders:** Jewelry, art, electronics, and collectibles scheduled individually
- **Identity Theft Protection:** Up to $25,000 in recovery costs
- **Equipment Breakdown Coverage:** Major appliances and home systems (HVAC, electrical panel)
- **Extended Replacement Cost:** Covers rebuilding costs even if they exceed policy limits by up to 25%

**Coverage Limits:**
- Dwelling: Up to $1,500,000
- Personal Property: Up to $500,000
- Liability: $500,000 per occurrence

**Monthly Starting Premium:** ~$210/month

---

## What Is NOT Covered (All Tiers)

- **Flooding** — Requires a separate flood insurance policy (available through NFIP or ShieldBase Flood add-on)
- **Earthquakes** — Requires a separate earthquake endorsement
- **Normal wear and tear or maintenance issues**
- **Mold and pest infestations** (unless caused by a covered peril)
- **Intentional damage** by the policyholder
- **Business equipment** used for commercial activity at home (requires home-based business endorsement)
- **Vacant properties** left unoccupied for more than 60 consecutive days without prior notice

## Home Insurance Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Dwelling Coverage** | Covers the physical structure of your home |
| **Personal Property** | Covers your belongings (furniture, electronics, clothes) |
| **Liability Coverage** | Pays if someone is injured on your property and sues you |
| **Loss of Use** | Covers hotel/living expenses if your home is uninhabitable |
| **Peril** | A specific risk or cause of loss (fire, wind, theft, etc.) |
| **Replacement Cost Value (RCV)** | Cost to replace item new, without depreciation |
| **Actual Cash Value (ACV)** | Replacement cost minus depreciation |

## Factors That Affect Your Home Premium

- **Location** — Proximity to fire stations, flood zones, and crime rates
- **Property type** — Single-family, condo, townhouse, manufactured home
- **Age and condition** of the home and roof
- **Estimated rebuild value** of the property
- **Claims history** — Prior claims may increase your premium
- **Security features** — Alarm systems, deadbolts, and smoke detectors can reduce rates
- **Coverage level and deductible chosen**
