# ShieldBase Life Insurance — Coverage Details

## Overview

Life insurance is the most important financial safety net you can give your family. ShieldBase offers term and whole life policies that are straightforward to understand, easy to apply for, and competitively priced. We help you choose the right coverage amount and term length for your unique situation.

## Product Types

### Term Life Insurance
Provides coverage for a fixed period (term). If the insured passes away during the term, beneficiaries receive the death benefit. If the term expires, coverage ends (or can be renewed at adjusted rates).

**Available Terms:** 10, 15, 20, or 30 years

**Coverage Amounts:** $100,000 to $5,000,000

**Best For:** Young families, mortgage protection, income replacement during working years

---

### Whole Life Insurance
Permanent coverage that lasts your entire life, as long as premiums are paid. Includes a cash value component that grows over time and can be borrowed against.

**Coverage Amounts:** $50,000 to $2,000,000

**Cash Value Growth Rate:** Guaranteed 3.5% annual growth

**Best For:** Estate planning, lifelong dependents, building a tax-advantaged savings vehicle

---

## Coverage Tiers (Term Life)

### Basic Term
- Death benefit paid as lump sum to beneficiaries
- Level premiums locked for the entire term
- Convertible to whole life at end of term (no new medical exam required)

**Sample Premium:** 35-year-old non-smoker, $500,000 / 20-year term ≈ $28/month

---

### Standard Term
Includes everything in Basic, plus:
- **Accelerated Death Benefit Rider:** Access up to 50% of death benefit early if diagnosed with terminal illness (12-month prognosis)
- **Waiver of Premium Rider:** Premiums waived if policyholder becomes totally disabled

**Sample Premium:** 35-year-old non-smoker, $500,000 / 20-year term ≈ $38/month

---

### Comprehensive Term
Includes everything in Standard, plus:
- **Child Term Rider:** Covers eligible dependent children up to age 25, $10,000–$25,000 per child
- **Accidental Death Benefit Rider:** Doubles the payout if death results from a qualifying accident
- **Return of Premium (optional):** If you outlive the term, 100% of premiums paid are refunded

**Sample Premium:** 35-year-old non-smoker, $500,000 / 20-year term ≈ $55/month

---

## What Affects Your Life Insurance Premium

- **Age** — Younger applicants receive significantly lower rates
- **Gender** — Statistically, women pay lower premiums due to longer life expectancy
- **Tobacco/Nicotine use** — Smokers typically pay 2–3x more than non-smokers
- **Health status** — BMI, blood pressure, cholesterol, and pre-existing conditions
- **Coverage amount** — Higher death benefit = higher premium
- **Term length** — Longer terms cost more
- **Family medical history** — Hereditary conditions may influence underwriting
- **Occupation and hobbies** — High-risk jobs or activities (e.g., skydiving) may increase rates

---

## Pre-Existing Conditions Policy

ShieldBase evaluates pre-existing conditions on a case-by-case basis during underwriting. Common conditions such as controlled hypertension, managed diabetes, or a history of cancer (in remission 5+ years) do not automatically disqualify applicants — they may result in a rated (adjusted-premium) policy.

**Conditions that may result in denial:**
- Actively undergoing cancer treatment
- Terminal diagnosis within the past 24 months
- Severe organ failure requiring transplant
- HIV/AIDS diagnosis (standard policies only; contact us for guaranteed-issue options)

---

## Beneficiaries

- You may name one or more primary and contingent beneficiaries
- Beneficiaries can be individuals, trusts, or charitable organizations
- You may change beneficiaries at any time by submitting a written request
- Death benefits paid to named beneficiaries are generally income-tax-free under current federal law

---

## What Is NOT Covered

- **Suicide** within the first 2 years of the policy (contestability period)
- **Fraud or material misrepresentation** on the application
- **Death while committing a felony**
- **Death in an act of war** (active military combat — separate coverage available through government programs)

---

## Application Process

1. Get a quote online or with an agent
2. Complete the health questionnaire
3. Schedule a free medical exam (required for coverage above $500,000)
4. Underwriting review: typically 5–15 business days
5. Policy issued and first premium collected
