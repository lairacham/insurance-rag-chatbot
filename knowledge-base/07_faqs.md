# ShieldBase Insurance — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## General Questions

**Q: How do I get a quote?**
A: You can get a quote online at www.shieldbaseinsurance.com/quote, by calling 1-800-743-5527, or by chatting with our AI assistant. Quotes for auto and home take about 5 minutes. Life insurance quotes may take slightly longer due to health questionnaire requirements.

**Q: Can I manage my policy online?**
A: Yes. The ShieldBase online portal and mobile app let you view your policy, update personal information, make payments, add or remove drivers/vehicles, and file claims — all without calling an agent.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: ShieldBase uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption and complies with all applicable state and federal data privacy laws including CCPA. We never sell your personal information to third parties.

**Q: Do you offer coverage in all 50 states?**
A: Yes. ShieldBase is licensed in all 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia.

## Eligibility Questions

**Q: Who is eligible for auto insurance?**
A: Any licensed U.S. driver aged 16 and older may apply. Drivers under 18 must have a parent or guardian co-sign the policy. High-risk drivers (multiple DUIs, license suspensions) may be subject to higher rates or limited coverage options.

**Q: Can I insure a home I don't live in?**
A: Yes, but a standard homeowners policy requires the property to be your primary residence. For vacation homes, rental properties, or investment properties, ask about our Specialty Property coverage.

**Q: Can I get life insurance if I have a pre-existing condition?**
A: In many cases, yes. ShieldBase evaluates pre-existing conditions individually. Common managed conditions (controlled diabetes, hypertension) do not automatically disqualify you — they may result in a slightly higher premium. See our Life Insurance Coverage document for details.

**Q: Is there a minimum age to purchase life insurance?**
A: Applicants must be at least 18 years old to purchase a policy. Children can be added as riders on a parent's policy (Child Term Rider, up to age 25).

## Cancellation & Changes

**Q: Can I cancel my policy at any time?**
A: Yes. You may cancel your policy at any time with no cancellation fee. If you prepaid your premium, ShieldBase will issue a pro-rated refund for the unused portion.

**Q: What happens if I miss a payment?**
A: ShieldBase provides a **10-day grace period** after your due date before coverage lapses. You'll receive an email and SMS reminder. If payment is not received by the end of the grace period, your policy will be suspended. You can reinstate within 30 days by paying the overdue amount, subject to a $25 reinstatement fee.

**Q: How do I add a new driver to my auto policy?**
A: Log in to the portal or call your agent. New drivers are typically added same-day. Premium adjustments take effect on the next billing cycle.

**Q: Can I change my coverage level mid-policy?**
A: Yes. You may upgrade or downgrade your coverage at any time. Changes take effect within 24 hours and your premium will be adjusted on a pro-rated basis.

## Claims Questions

**Q: How long does it take to process a claim?**
A: Most claims are resolved within 3–5 business days. Complex claims (major property damage, disputed liability) may take up to 30 days. You can track your claim status in real time through the portal.

**Q: Will filing a claim raise my premium?**
A: It depends on the type and frequency of claims. A single not-at-fault accident or weather-related home claim typically does not affect your premium. At-fault accidents or multiple claims within a 3-year period may result in a rate adjustment at renewal.

**Q: Do I need a police report to file a claim?**
A: A police report is required for theft, vandalism, hit-and-run accidents, and any incident involving criminal activity. For other claims (e.g., a single-car collision or weather damage), a police report is not required but may be helpful.

## Bundling & Discounts

**Q: What discounts are available?**
A: ShieldBase offers a wide range of discounts including: safe driver, good student, multi-vehicle, home security, claims-free, loyalty, defensive driving, and bundling. Ask your agent which discounts you qualify for — most customers save 10–25% after discounts are applied.

**Q: How does bundling work?**
A: When you hold two or more ShieldBase policies (e.g., auto + home), you automatically qualify for the bundle discount. Discounts are applied to each policy's base premium and show up on your billing statement.

**Q: Can I bundle policies that started at different times?**
A: Yes. You can add a second policy at any time and start receiving the bundle discount immediately. Your existing policy will be retroactively discounted from the date the second policy takes effect.

## Auto-Specific Questions

**Q: What's the difference between comprehensive and collision coverage?**
A: Collision covers damage to your car from an accident with another vehicle or object. Comprehensive covers non-collision damage — theft, fire, hail, flooding, hitting an animal, and vandalism. Full coverage means having both.

**Q: Does my auto policy cover rental cars?**
A: Standard and Comprehensive plans include rental reimbursement while your car is being repaired. This does not automatically extend to rental cars you use for leisure travel — you can add a rental car endorsement for that purpose.

**Q: Am I covered if someone else drives my car?**
A: Yes, in most cases. ShieldBase covers permissive use — meaning anyone you give permission to drive your vehicle is covered under your policy. Excluded drivers (listed on your policy) are never covered.
